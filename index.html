ABOUT PROJECT OGYES

The Ogun State Government is positioned to upgrade the skills and knowledge of its populace as part of the ISEYA Focus. As a step towards Youth Empowerment, his Excellency, Prince Dapo Abiodun has launched the Ogun State Youth Empowerment Scheme (OG-YES). The focus of the Scheme is to give indigenes of Ogun State the opportunity to:

  • Secure workplacement opportunities in Private and Public Institutions across the state
  • Ensure the Youth learn a Skill of choice through hands-on and on the job training
  • Get paid a stipend as a support during the learning process
  • Also get paid by the company/agency.

The number of candidates enrolled into the program would be initially 5000 with a plan to expand it over the next couple of quarters.
